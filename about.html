Who am I?

Welcome to my site! My name is Bernice and I’m someone who loves creating things that feel cozy, intentional, and a little bit fun. Outside of the kitchen, I’m always experimenting — whether that’s trying new hobbies, making things look aesthetic, or just learning whatever catches my brain that week.

This space is basically my little corner of the internet where I get to share what I’ve made, how I’ve grown, and the things that inspire me. Hope you enjoy exploring around.

My Story

My story in the kitchen started when I was six, standing on a little stool so I could actually reach the counter. I learned most of what I know by watching the people around me — my mom, my nanny, my grandmas, and honestly any elder who was down to let me hover over their shoulder while they cooked.

They taught me the basics, the tricks, the little unspoken things that never show up in recipes. And as I got older, I started branching out on my own — trying new cuisines, experimenting with flavors, and figuring out what kind of food feels like ‘me.’

Cooking has always been this mix of family, curiosity, and creativity for me, and it’s something I’ve carried with me ever since.
